Movie profit as a function of audience composition.

Data-Driven Audience Targeting to Maximize Movie Profitability

2nd Place, Citadel East Coast Regional Datathon ($2,500 cash prize)

How can movie producers identify the optimal target audience in order to maximize profit? Using k-means clustering, we found that movie viewers can be grouped into three primary types: comedy lovers, drama lovers, and viewers with wide/eclectic tastes. We then grouped movies based on their audience composition and found that movies with audiences comprised of 20% comedy lovers, 20% drama lovers, and 60% viewers with wide/eclectic tastes earn the highest profit on average (p < 0.001). We also find the ideal audience for movies of each genre.
